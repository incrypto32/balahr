Who we are

About Us

An independently owned private agency based in Dubai- Bala HR Consultancy has been successfully providing permanent staff throughout the public and private sectors in and around the Greater UAE area. As an independent company, we are flexible, offering bespoke services to our candidates and clients. Our professionally tailored approach allows us to deliver high levels of customer-focused service and maintaining excellent long-standing relationships with our candidates and clients alike. We place skilled professionals across a range of specifications

Our Vision

To be the most efficient, profitable, respected,executive search and selection HR Consulting firm of choice in the market place. To pro vide comprehensive, value added, executive recruitment services to the clients through a well-rewarded, dedicated team who work in an enjoyable and challenging working environment.

Our Mission

We have a two-fold mission. We help companies hire the best administrative and human resources staff to help meet organizational goals and cultivate a highly engaged, productive workforce. We also help job seekers find fulfilling employment with valuable companies.

Mode Of Operation

  • We develop a job profile for your approval for use when talking to candidates.

  • We will build a candidate shortlist from our part time committee.

  • We arrange interviews and brief the candidates you select.

  • If several strong candidates emerge , we arrange a second interview.

  • We support the contracting process with your preferred candidates.

  • We follow up a few weeks after the start date to make sure everything is going smoothly.
